About Gegson Nig Ltd

Gegson Nig Ltd has been operating in Nigeria for over 30 years, providing multidisciplinary engineering and environmental services, such as Water Engineering, mechanical engineering, construction and project management, chemical and consultancy services to support our clients across a wide spectrum of industries; and to ultimately ensure cost-effective and total environmental and legal compliance.

As one of the leading reputable firm in the industry we pride ourselves with a dedicated and experienced team that focus on high quality, interdisciplinary and innovative consulting, design and construction solutions while placing our team and client relations at the heart of the business.
